# Final Quiz — Session 13: Advanced Topics in Cybersecurity

**Instructions:** Choose the single best answer for each question.

---

**Question 1:** A developer at your company accidentally set an S3 bucket to `public-read-write`.
What is the MOST serious risk this creates?

A) The bucket consumes more storage than allocated
B) Anyone on the internet can upload malware or download confidential files
C) The bucket's encryption is automatically disabled
D) The bucket becomes inaccessible to internal users

**Correct Answer: B**

---

**Question 2:** In STIX 2.1, what is a "Relationship" object used for?

A) Describing a specific IP address or file hash indicator
B) Linking two STIX Domain Objects (e.g., connecting a Threat Actor to a Malware object)
C) Specifying which TAXII server distributes the intelligence
D) Defining the Traffic Light Protocol marking for the bundle

**Correct Answer: B**

---

**Question 3:** A Zero Trust policy requires "device posture checks." What does this mean?

A) Checking that the user's physical location is within the office building
B) Verifying that the accessing device meets security requirements (patched OS, EDR running, disk encrypted)
C) Requiring users to physically show their badge to a camera before access is granted
D) Scanning for hardware keyloggers on the device

**Correct Answer: B**

---

**Question 4:** During a purple team exercise, the red team successfully executes Mimikatz to dump LSASS credentials without triggering any SIEM alert.
What does this finding indicate?

A) The SOC's network monitoring is insufficient
B) The red team violated the rules of engagement
C) There is a detection gap for credential dumping that must be remediated
D) This is expected and acceptable because Mimikatz is only used in targeted attacks

**Correct Answer: C**

---

**Question 5:** Under the GDPR, when must an organisation notify the supervisory authority of a personal data breach?

A) Within 24 hours
B) Within 72 hours of becoming aware
C) Within 30 days
D) Immediately, before any containment actions

**Correct Answer: B**

---

**Question 6:** The Harvest Now, Decrypt Later (HNDL) attack strategy is specifically relevant to which emerging threat?

A) AI-generated phishing emails
B) Supply chain attacks against software vendors
C) Quantum computing's future ability to break current asymmetric encryption
D) Ransomware attacks against cloud storage

**Correct Answer: C**

---

**Question 7:** What is a Software Bill of Materials (SBOM) and why is it important for supply chain security?

A) An invoice for software licenses that lists costs per component
B) A formal inventory of all software components and dependencies, used to identify vulnerable or malicious components
C) A backup of the software source code stored securely off-site
D) A checklist of software tests that must pass before deployment

**Correct Answer: B**
