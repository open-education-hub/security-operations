# Entry Quiz — Session 13: Advanced Topics in Cybersecurity

**Instructions:** Choose the single best answer for each question.

---

**Question 1:** In the cloud shared responsibility model, which of the following is ALWAYS the customer's responsibility regardless of the service type (IaaS, PaaS, or SaaS)?

A) Operating system patching
B) Physical hardware security
C) Data and identity management
D) Hypervisor security

**Correct Answer: C**

---

**Question 2:** What does "Zero Trust" mean in the context of network security?

A) No users are trusted until they pay a security deposit
B) All traffic inside the network perimeter is trusted; only external traffic is checked
C) No implicit trust is granted based on network location; every access request is verified
D) Only zero-day vulnerability attacks are considered threats

**Correct Answer: C**

---

**Question 3:** What is STIX in the context of threat intelligence?

A) A programming language used to write SIEM detection rules
B) A structured format for describing and sharing cyber threat information
C) A firewall configuration standard for blocking known threats
D) A government regulation for incident notification

**Correct Answer: B**

---

**Question 4:** What does MTTD stand for in SOC metrics?

A) Maximum Time to Deploy
B) Mean Time to Detect
C) Minimum Time to Defend
D) Managed Threat and Tactics Database

**Correct Answer: B**

---

**Question 5:** Which EU regulation imposes incident notification requirements specifically on essential and important sectors such as healthcare, energy, and finance?

A) GDPR
B) PCI-DSS
C) NIS2 Directive
D) ISO 27001

**Correct Answer: C**
